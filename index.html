About Us

The LAP – Laboratory of Antennas and Propagation, located at Technological Institute of Aeronautics, has a strong commitment to training undergraduate and graduate students in topics related to the planar and conformal microstrip antennas, microstrip phased arrays and direction-of-arrival estimation, with special emphasis on aerospace and aeronautical technologies.

The LAP's research infrastructure is composed of:

  • Computational laboratory that has computing platforms and various electromagnetic simulation software packages to support antenna design.
  • Prototyping laboratory which provides a PCB prototyping machine, a diversity of microwave laminates and microwave components.
  • Measurement laboratory that is well equipped with network analyzer, signal analyzer, spectrum analyzer, microwave generator, oscilloscope and power supply.
